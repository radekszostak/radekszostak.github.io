Semantic segmentation of water bodies on satellite imagery

About

A brand new dataset containing labelled images was prepared for the purpose of this work. Labelling was performed in semiautomated way with help of OpenCV. Semantic segmentation is common application of convolutional neural networks. Nevertheless, project implementation was mirrored in both PyTorch and TensorFlow, which allowed for in practice comparition between two most popular deep learning frameworks.
